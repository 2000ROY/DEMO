The government's intention behind the launch of a physical store in New Delhi, the capital city, and consequently in other online platforms and the rest of the country is to have a one-stop market space for buyers, curators, and art enthusiasts, and to convert years and months of arduous labour that have been spent in looking for platforms to retain primitive traditions and cultures by artists alike by the important tribes in Meghalaya

Jompoi

Jompoi enables the consumers in purchasing farm-fresh products of high quality at reasonable prices, which is delivered to their ...doorstep. The farmers can upload their products in the app. This essentially removes the middleman thereby reducing the delivery time as well as fresh products to the consumers at a reasonable price. Also, farmers get better returns since they are selling directly to the customers
